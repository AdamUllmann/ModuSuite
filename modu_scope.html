modu-SCOPE - Lightweight Audio Visualizer

Click here for the GitHub

Features

  • Support for Standalone and VST Plugin Formats: Utilize modu-SCOPE as a standalone application or integrate it into your favorite DAW as a VST plugin.
  • Oscilloscope: Visualize the audio waveform in the time domain.
  • Spectrogram: Visualize the audio frequencies over time using a spectrogram.
  • Real-time visual audio Processing with Low Latency: Experience responsive visual audio processing with minimal latency.

Usage

  • Open your audio software and create a new project.
  • Add modu-SCOPE to a track or channel in your project.
  • Play audio in your project, and you should see the waveform in the plugin UI.
